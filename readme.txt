Display your Survey Advantage Reviews on your WordPress installation!

== Description ==

This plugin eases the installation of Survey Advantage's Testimonial Publisher into your
WordPress-powered website enabling full-integration in less than two-minutes!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `survey-advantage-reviews` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Enter Feed ID on `Survey Advantage Reviews` tab that now appears in the navigation menu on the left.
1. Insert shortcode on your testimonials page ether manual or with `Step 2` under the `Survey Advantage Reviews` tab.
1. Done - sit back and enjoy the benefits!
